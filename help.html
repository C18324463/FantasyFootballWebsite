FAQ

Q. How do I start making a team?
A. You need to create an account to make a team.
Q. How do I create an account?
A. Click the Register button at the top of the page and follow the instructions there.
Q. Can I change my team after I pick my players and submit my team?
A. No, once you have chosen your 11 players, those are your players for the whole season.
Q. What happens if I don't want to create an account?
A. If you don't create an account, you can't make a team. You will only be able to look at statistics, fixtures and results of matches.
Q. What happens if one of my players is injured, suspended or doesn't play?
A. He'll score you 0 points, unlucky pal.
Q. What happens if VAR changes a decison on a goal or yellow/red card?
A. The points will be changed according in your team.
